Cherry is currently in beta. It works as a prototype, but the interface, mobile UX, scheduling model, and release flow are still being refined.

Why Cherry exists

A todo app for people who think in structure, not just lists.

Cherryの中心は「やることの流れ」です。大きな作業をRoot taskとして置き、そこから実際にやる小さなタスクを枝のようにつなげていきます。

01

Flow board

Build a project visually from a root task, then extend child tasks as branches.

02

Date lanes

Use dates as helpful anchors without letting the calendar become the whole structure.

03

Direct interaction

Create and connect tasks close to where you are working, instead of opening huge modals for everything.

04

Open-source friendly

Cherry is being prepared as a small, understandable project that contributors can improve.

Beta, not final

Still growing. Very much on purpose.

Stable enough to try

The beta is useful for exploring the flow-first idea and testing the basic board behavior.

Not yet final

Mobile UX, scheduling details, list view, release notes, and support links are still planned or under discussion.
